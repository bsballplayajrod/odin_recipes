Cranberry Sauce

Description

This cranberry sauce recipe uses fresh cranberries, sugar, and orange juice to make a Thanksgiving classic.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cranberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Dissolve sugar in orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Stir in cranberries and cook until they start to pop, about 8 to 10 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. It will thicken as it cools
  5. Enjoy!
